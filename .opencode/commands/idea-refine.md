# Idea Refine

Refine a raw idea into a sharp, actionable concept through structured divergent and convergent thinking.

## Usage

Provide your raw idea and I will guide you through three phases:

1. **Understand & Expand** — Restate as a "How Might We" problem, ask sharpening questions, generate 5-8 variations
2. **Evaluate & Converge** — Cluster resonating ideas, stress-test against user value/feasibility/differentiation, surface hidden assumptions
3. **Sharpen & Ship** — Produce a concrete markdown one-pager with problem statement, recommended direction, key assumptions, MVP scope, and "Not Doing" list

## Trigger

"Help me refine this idea: [your idea]"
"Ideate on [concept]"
"Stress-test my plan"

## Output

A markdown one-pager saved to `docs/ideas/[idea-name].md` (after your confirmation).
